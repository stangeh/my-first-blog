Recipe: The Best Chocolate Cake In The World

This should be kept a secret, because it is our secret weapon at all parties. Combine 350 g flour, 500 g sugar, 200 g cocoa powder, 200 g milk, then bake at 180º for 30 mins. Slather in World's Best Chocolate Frosting, then shove your face in it.
